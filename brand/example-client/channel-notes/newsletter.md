# Channel Fine-Tuning: Newsletter
# Meridian (Monthly)

---

## Newsletter strategy

### Frequency
Monthly digest for ops leaders and founders thinking about operations.

### Audience
People who've shown interest in Meridian (website visitors, LinkedIn followers, past audit participants).

### Goal
Thought leadership and education. Keep Meridian top-of-mind without constant selling.

---

## Newsletter structure

**Format:**
1. Headline insight (one powerful idea, 150 words)
2. Case study or example (300 words)
3. Quick tool or framework (200 words)
4. What we're thinking about (100 words)

**Length:** 750–1000 words total

**Tone:** Company voice (we, Meridian), but with Alex's directness. Same principles as blog, but tighter.

---

## Subject line style

Examples of good subject lines:
- "Why your automation project will fail (and what to do instead)"
- "The $500K cost of one bad process decision"

What doesn't work:
- Clickbait ("You won't believe...")
- Overly clever
- Jargon-heavy

---

## CTA approach

Soft call-to-action: "If you're working through this, reply and let me know what you're seeing."
No aggressive "book a demo." Just inviting conversation.
