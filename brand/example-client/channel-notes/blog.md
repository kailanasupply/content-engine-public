# Channel Fine-Tuning: Blog
# Meridian

---

## Blog strategy

### Purpose
Sales enablement and thought leadership. Help operations leaders solve real problems, which naturally brings prospects to Meridian.

### Publishing frequency
2x per month. One post tied to a content pillar, one evergreen.

### Target audience
Operations leaders at 50–500 person companies (primary), founders considering operations investment (secondary).

---

## Blog post format

### Length
1,200–2,000 words. Deep enough to be genuinely useful.

### Structure
- Hook (specific situation or insight): 100 words
- Problem statement: 200 words
- Framework or approach: 600 words
- Case study / example: 400 words
- Key takeaway: 200 words
- CTA: soft invitation to audit

### SEO approach
Target keywords like "operations bottleneck," "automation strategy," "scaling operations."
Use H2 and H3 headings. Meta description is a specific takeaway, not sales copy.

---

## Blog post categories

1. **Diagnostic frameworks:** "How to identify your real operations bottleneck"
2. **Automation strategy:** "What to automate (and what not to)"
3. **Scaling operations:** "Operations that don't break at 2x growth"
4. **Case studies:** "How a 150-person company cut ops overhead 40%"

---

## Tone and voice

Company voice throughout. Same directness as LinkedIn, more depth. "We" and "Meridian" language. Teaching, not selling.

---

## CTA strategy

End with: "If you're working through a similar challenge, we do a free 1-hour bottleneck audit. We'll tell you what we'd do — no pitch, just advice."
