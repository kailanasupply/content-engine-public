# Channel Fine-Tuning: Company LinkedIn
# Meridian

---

## Company page strategy

### Content categories
- Client case studies (anonymized)
- Meridian announcements (hires, partnerships)
- Thought leadership on operations scaling
- Team updates and company milestones

### Posting frequency
1–2x per week. Deliberate, not constant. Quality over volume.

### Tone difference from founder's personal posts
More polished. Client-focused instead of personal-experience-focused.
Same directness, but with a company voice. "We've found" instead of "I've seen."

---

## Post format rules

### Length range
100–200 words. Short, tight, and purposeful.

### Hook style
Lead with outcome or insight. "We helped a company cut payment processing time by 95%. Here's how they did it."

### Visual approach
Minimal graphics. Text-heavy LinkedIn carousel if necessary.

### Hashtags
1–2 if it fits naturally. #OperationsLeadership #Scaling

---

## Company content categories

1. **Client results (anonymized):** Problem → approach → results → lesson
2. **Operations insights:** Contrarian takes on what works in operations
3. **Company updates:** Hiring, partnerships, company milestones

---

## What NOT to post on company page

- Personal stories about Alex's past failures (belongs on personal LinkedIn)
- Political or controversial takes (unless directly about operations)
- Daily life or team random updates
