## Personal vs. Company Voice: Quick Reference

| Element | Personal (Founder) | Company |
|---------|-------------------|---------|
| Pronoun | I | We |
| Anecdotes | Personal life, personal failures | Client stories, company milestones |
| Uncertainty | "I'm not sure, but..." | Rarely expressed — brand speaks from authority |
| Opinions | Hot takes, personal stances | Positioned POV — stronger, more considered |
| Length (LinkedIn) | Longer personal essays common | Tighter, more purposeful |
| Humor | Personal humor | Warmer, more universal — never edgy |
| Frequency of posts | Higher — personal brand | Lower — more deliberate |
