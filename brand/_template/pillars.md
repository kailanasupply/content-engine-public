## Content Pillar Mapping Rules

When the orchestrator assigns a pillar to a piece of content, it should follow these rules:

1. **One pillar per content piece** — no double-tagging
2. **If it spans two pillars** — pick the one that is more prominent in the content
3. **If it fits no pillar** — flag it; do not generate or publish
4. **Distribution target** — aim for roughly equal coverage across pillars over time (use the content calendar to track balance)
