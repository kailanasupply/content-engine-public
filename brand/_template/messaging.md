## Messaging Rules for Generators

1. **Always tie content back to at least one core value prop** — even subtly
2. **Use proof points whenever making a claim** — don't just assert, evidence it
3. **Never use competitor names** in content unless it's a deliberate, strategic comparison piece
4. **The "before state" is more powerful than the "after state"** — lead with the pain
5. **Specificity beats generality** — "37% faster" beats "much faster"
