## Personal vs. Company Voice: The Key Differences

When generating content for this person's **personal** voice vs. their **company** voice,
apply these specific differences:

| Element | Personal Voice | Company Voice |
|---------|---------------|---------------|
| Pronoun | I / me / my | We / our / the team |
| Tone register | Conversational, raw | Polished, authoritative |
| Stories | Personal — things that happened to them | Case studies, client outcomes |
| Opinions | Direct first-person takes | Positioned brand POV |
| Vulnerability | Present — they can be wrong, uncertain | Rare — brand speaks with authority |
| CTAs | Soft — invite conversation | Clear — drive action |
| Humor | Their specific humor | Warmer but more controlled |
| Disclosure | Personal life can bleed in | Business-focused only |
