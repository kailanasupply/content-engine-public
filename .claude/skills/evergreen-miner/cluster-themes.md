# Cluster Themes

Defines recurring idea clusters per client. When the evergreen miner surfaces two or more ideas from the same cluster, it notes the campaign opportunity.

---

## brian

### Cluster: AI-and-fraud
**Pillar:** fraud-leadership + ai-disruption
**Core tension:** AI is rewriting what fraud leadership looks like — but most fraud teams are still running old playbooks
**Constituent ideas:**
- First 90 days running fraud with AI
- Will AI take my fraud job? Wrong question.
- Every vendor claims AI — one question that separates them
- Is your fraud AI doing anything your rules couldn't?
- The tools are ahead of the teams
**Campaign opportunity:** 3+ ideas here → could anchor a "Fraud Leadership in the AI Era" mini-series (personal LinkedIn + newsletter)

### Cluster: cost-vs-strategy
**Pillar:** fraud-as-strategy
**Core tension:** Fraud is still treated as a cost center when the data shows it's a growth lever
**Constituent ideas:**
- Fraud is a cost center because you designed it that way
- Safeguard practitioner reimbursement — doubled
- Holding the Head of Fraud title vs. leading a fraud program
**Campaign opportunity:** 2+ ideas → "From Cost Center to Competitive Edge" angle available

### Cluster: building-in-public
**Pillar:** building-in-public
**Core tension:** Building real things in the open, showing the messy parts, not just the wins
**Constituent ideas:**
- HOF job board overnight rebuild
- Three projects building in public
- 18-month transformation timeline (personal version)
**Campaign opportunity:** Ongoing series — pairs well with founder story content

### Cluster: trust-and-customers
**Pillar:** trust
**Core tension:** The hardest fraud to stop comes from customers — which means stopping fraud requires understanding when to trust
**Constituent ideas:**
- The hardest fraud to stop is coming from your own customers
- What do you call it when your real customer lies to you?
- First-party fraud framing
**Campaign opportunity:** 2+ ideas → could anchor a "Trust Paradox" series
